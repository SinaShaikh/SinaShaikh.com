About

Hi my name is Sina Shaikh, and I work at MIT in the Elections Lab. I recently graduated Summa Cum Laude from the University of Pennsylvania with a Bachelor of Science in Engineering
in computer science and minors in political science and survey research and data analytics. Over the past few years I've worked as a research fellow through the Penn Program on Opinion Research and Election Studies (PORES), for the Brookings Institution, for the Perry World House, as a TA for CIS 110 and GAFL 5310, and on the Decision Desk for NBC News.
